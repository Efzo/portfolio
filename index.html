About Efosa Ojomo - Software Engineer | Site Reliability Engineer | Product Specialist

Efosa Ojomo holds a Bachelor's degree in Electrical Electronics Engineering and two Master's degrees, one in Computer Electronics System Engineering and another in Computer Technology from Eastern Illinois University.

With this strong academic foundation, Efosa has built a career as a Software Engineer, Site Reliability Engineer, and Product Specialist, bringing over 8+ years of experience in developing robust applications, ensuring system reliability, and driving product excellence. He specializes in building scalable software systems, implementing SRE best practices, and delivering products that meet both technical and business objectives.

As a Software Engineer, Efosa has extensive experience in full-stack development, working with modern programming languages, frameworks, and technologies. He develops clean, maintainable code and builds systems that are scalable, efficient, and user-friendly. His expertise spans frontend and backend development, API design, database architecture, and cloud-native application development.

In his role as a Site Reliability Engineer, Efosa focuses on ensuring the reliability, availability, and performance of production systems. He implements infrastructure automation, builds monitoring and observability solutions, manages incident response, and works to eliminate toil through automation. His SRE practices include capacity planning, performance optimization, disaster recovery, and implementing SLOs/SLIs to maintain high system reliability.

As a Product Specialist, Efosa bridges the gap between engineering and business, working closely with stakeholders to define product requirements, prioritize features, and ensure successful product delivery. He is a certified Scrum Master and Product Owner who excels at translating business needs into technical solutions and driving product strategy that delivers measurable value.

Efosa's passion lies in building reliable, scalable systems that solve real problems. He stays current with industry trends in software engineering, SRE practices, DevOps, cloud technologies, and product management, constantly expanding his skills to deliver exceptional value to users and organizations.

Currently based in Austin, Texas, Efosa continues to contribute to the tech community through his expertise in software engineering, site reliability engineering, and product management. His proven track record in delivering reliable, scalable software systems demonstrates his commitment to technical excellence and operational reliability.
