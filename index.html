About Efosa Ojomo - Software Engineer | Cloud Solution Architect | Technical Product Specialist

Efosa Ojomo holds a Bachelor's degree in Electrical Electronics Engineering and two Master's degrees, one in Computer Electronics System Engineering and another in Computer Technology from Eastern Illinois University.

With this strong academic foundation, Efosa began his journey as a Software Engineer, building robust applications and developing deep technical expertise. Over 8+ years, he has evolved into a Cloud Solution Architect and Technical Product Specialist, bringing a unique combination of hands-on development experience, architectural vision, and product strategy expertise to every project.

Starting as a Software Engineer, Efosa developed expertise in full-stack development, understanding both frontend and backend technologies. This foundation enabled him to transition into Cloud Solution Architecture, where he designs and implements scalable cloud-native solutions using AWS, Azure, and multi-cloud architectures. His deep technical background allows him to make informed architectural decisions that balance performance, scalability, and cost.

As a Technical Product Specialist, Efosa bridges the gap between technical implementation and business strategy. He is a certified Scrum Master, Product Owner, and Agile Coach who specializes in translating business requirements into technical solutions, managing product roadmaps, and leading cross-functional teams. His unique ability to speak both technical and business languages makes him invaluable in driving product success.

Efosa's passion lies in creating solutions that combine technical excellence with business value. He is dedicated to staying at the forefront of technology trends, constantly expanding his knowledge in areas like serverless computing, cloud security, microservices architecture, and product management to deliver exceptional value.

Currently based in Austin, Texas, Efosa works as a Cloud Solution Architect and Technical Product Specialist, continuing to contribute to the tech community through his expertise in software engineering, cloud technologies, and technical product management.
