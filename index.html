About Efosa Ojomo - Cloud Solution Architect

Efosa Ojomo holds a Bachelor's degree in Electrical Electronics Engineering and two Master's degrees—one in Computer Electronics System Engineering and another in Computer Technology from Eastern Illinois University.

With this strong academic foundation, Efosa has specialized in Cloud Solution Architecture and digital transformation. As a seasoned Cloud Solution Architect, Software Engineer, and Technical Product Specialist, Efosa brings 7+ years of experience in designing, developing, and implementing cloud-native solutions that drive innovation and scalability. His expertise spans AWS, Azure, and multi-cloud architectures, enabling him to bridge the gap between business requirements and cutting-edge cloud technologies effectively.

In addition to his cloud architecture expertise, Efosa is a certified Scrum Master, Product Owner, Product Manager, Project Manager, and Agile Coach. He specializes in cloud migration strategies, microservices architecture, containerization, and DevOps practices.

Efosa's passion lies in architecting robust, scalable cloud solutions that enable digital transformation and business growth. He is dedicated to staying at the forefront of cloud technology trends and best practices, constantly expanding his knowledge in areas like serverless computing, cloud security, and infrastructure as code to deliver exceptional value to clients and organizations.

Currently based in Austin, Texas, Efosa works as a Cloud Solution Architect and continues to contribute to the tech community through his expertise in cloud technologies, software engineering, and technical product management.
